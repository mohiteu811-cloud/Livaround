01 📊 Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is the first screen you see after logging in. It gives you a real-time overview of your entire operation — properties, bookings, pending jobs, worker activity, revenue, and stock alerts — all without navigating away.

  1. Log in at app.livaround.com with your host credentials.
  2. Review the 8 KPI cards at the top for an instant health check.
  3. Check the Revenue Trend chart to spot month-on-month growth.
  4. Act on Low Stock Alerts and Pending Jobs before they become problems.
  5. Click any row in the Recent Bookings or Upcoming Jobs tables to jump straight to that record.

02 🏠 Properties

Properties are the foundation of LivAround. Every booking, job, worker assignment, inventory item and revenue report is linked to a property. Set them up once with full details — including WiFi, door codes and house rules — and the platform uses that information everywhere.

  1. Go to Properties in the sidebar and click + Add Property.
  2. Fill in name, type, address, bedrooms, bathrooms and amenities.
  3. Enter Guest Experience details: WiFi credentials, check-in instructions, house rules.
  4. Optionally link or create a Property Owner with an involvement level.
  5. Click Save Property. Use the Guide and Staff quick-links on the card to continue setup.

03 📅 Bookings

The Bookings section tracks every guest stay across all your properties. Log bookings from Airbnb, Booking.com, direct and other channels in one place. Each booking generates a unique guest stay link with WiFi details, house rules and check-in instructions.

  1. Go to Bookings and click + Add Booking.
  2. Select the property and booking source (Airbnb, Direct, Booking.com, VRBO, LivAround).
  3. Enter guest name, email, phone and the check-in / check-out dates.
  4. Set the total amount, currency and optionally a lock code.
  5. When the guest arrives, click the Check In icon. On departure, click Check Out.

04 💼 Jobs & Dispatch

Jobs are tasks you assign to workers — cleaning, cooking, driving or maintenance. Create a job, customise its checklist, and dispatch it to an available worker. Workers receive a push notification on the mobile app and can accept, start and complete jobs in real time.

  1. Go to Jobs and click + Create Job.
  2. Select a property, job type and scheduled date/time. Optionally link to a booking.
  3. Review and customise the default checklist for that job type.
  4. Click Dispatch — select an available worker with the matching skill.
  5. Monitor status updates (Dispatched → Accepted → In Progress → Completed) in real time.
  6. Archive completed jobs to keep your active list clean.

05 👥 Workers

Workers are the people who carry out your jobs — cleaners, cooks, drivers and maintenance staff. Add them to the platform, assign them to properties, and manage their availability and skills from one screen. Workers log in via the mobile app or web portal.

  1. Go to Workers and click + Add Worker.
  2. Enter their name, email, phone, location and select at least one skill.
  3. Click Create Worker — a temporary password is shown. Share it with the worker.
  4. Use the availability toggle to control whether they receive new dispatch notifications.
  5. Click the property assign button to link them to one or more properties and set their role (Cleaner, Caretaker, Supervisor).

06 📦 Inventory

Track consumable stock across all your properties — cleaning supplies, toiletries, kitchen items and linens. Set minimum thresholds and LivAround will alert you (and show on the Dashboard) when stock falls below the safe level, so you never run out between guest stays.

  1. Go to Inventory and click + Add Item.
  2. Select the property, category, item name, current stock, minimum threshold and unit.
  3. Optionally upload photos and enter the storage location.
  4. When stock is replenished, click the Restock icon and enter the new quantity.
  5. Use the Low Stock Only toggle to filter and action alerts quickly.

07 ⚠ Issues

Issues are problems reported by workers during or after a job — a broken appliance, missing supplies, damage, or a safety concern. Workers submit them directly from the mobile app with photos and a severity level. You review and resolve them from this screen.

  1. Go to Issues to see all worker-reported problems.
  2. Filter by status (Open, In Review, Resolved) or severity (Low, Medium, High).
  3. Click any issue card to open the detail panel — read the full description and view attached photos or video.
  4. Click Mark In Review to acknowledge it, or Resolve once actioned.
  5. For high-severity issues, consider creating a Maintenance Request to assign a tradesperson.

08 🔨 Maintenance Requests

Maintenance Requests are raised by on-site caretakers or cleaners when something needs repair. Each request includes a description, priority level, trade role required and an optional photo. You review and approve (or reject) before a tradesperson is assigned.

  1. On-site staff submits a request via the worker portal — you receive a notification.
  2. Go to Maintenance and find the new Pending request.
  3. Click Review — read the description, trade role and priority.
  4. Click Approve: optionally assign a worker, set a scheduled date and add notes.
  5. Or click Reject with a reason note sent back to the caretaker.
  6. Track the request through to Completed status.

09 💲 Revenue Reports

Revenue Reports give owners a transparent breakdown of monthly earnings, platform fees, your commission, and expenses — with a clear Net to Owner figure. Import directly from Airbnb CSV or enter figures manually. Published reports appear in the owner's portal.

  1. Go to Revenue and click + Add Report.
  2. Select property, month and year. Upload the Airbnb earnings CSV to auto-fill the figures.
  3. Verify gross revenue, platform fees and net revenue. Set the commission %.
  4. Add any expenses (cleaning, repairs, etc.) with amounts and optional receipts.
  5. Change status from Draft to Published to share with the owner.

10 📘 Operations Guide & Tradesmen

The Operations Guide is a per-property knowledge base for your on-site staff. Organise documentation by room or area, add instructions with photos, and generate QR code labels to stick near appliances. Workers can access it any time from the app — so they always know where things are and how they work.

  1. Go to Properties → click Guide on the property card.
  2. Click + Add Area and name a zone (Master Bedroom, Pool Area, Utility Room...).
  3. Inside an area, click + Add Doc — enter title, category, instructions and upload photos.
  4. Click Print QR Label on any doc to download a QR sticker — attach it near the item.
  5. Add Vendor Contacts (electrician, plumber, AC tech) in the Contacts tab. Use Save to Tradesmen to add them to the central database.

11 📍 Live Worker Tracking

The Tracking screen shows the last known GPS location of all your workers on an interactive map. Locations update every 15 seconds. This lets you confirm a worker has arrived at a property, monitor progress across multiple sites, and respond quickly if someone is delayed.

  1. Go to Tracking in the sidebar.
  2. Workers with a green dot have shared their location; grey dot means no data.
  3. Click a worker in the left sidebar to centre the map on their position.
  4. The map refreshes automatically every 15 seconds. Use the manual refresh button for an instant update.
  5. Workers must grant Always location permission in the app for continuous tracking.
